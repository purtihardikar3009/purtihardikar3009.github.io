Lets Start the game

A laugh challenge game to play with your friends. The game is developed using artificial intelligence to detect your smile and laugh using landmarks. A score analyses will tell you if you win or lose!
Excited, let us get started!

*Terms and Conditions: To interact with this game, you must allow access to your camera. No image data is sent to our servers; all images are sorted on your device. The photos are private and aren't used in any way. You can even play with hiding the video and only using the outline of your lips using the incognito mode.
